Banana Smoothie

This is a simple smoothie recipe that's great for a quick breakfast!

Ingredients:

  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1/4 cup rolled oats
  • 2 tbs peanut butter
  • 1tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 scoop protein powder
  • 1 cup milk

Instructions:

  1. Put all ingredients into the blender.
  2. Blend until contents are consistent
  3. Pour into a cup and serve.
